SALVAGE
Shoot junk for scrap, then fly through the wreck — it reels in on its own. Ease off the throttle to collect; a boosting ship outruns the beam. Ram anything and you spill cargo — swing back to scoop it up before it drifts off. Land anywhere to repair the hull.
Cargo in the hold can spill; the BANK can't. Park on the Charleston pad — the gold HOME marker points the way — and your hold pays out. Banked scrap survives a wreck, and a reload.
While docked, the OUTFITTER sells upgrades — beam range, hull plating, cargo clamps. Click a row, press 1 / 2 / 3, or D-pad + A on a gamepad.
